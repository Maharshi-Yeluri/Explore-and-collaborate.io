Ran across this post and I have to say, I am inspired by it. I am a lowly Transportation Coordinator in a public school district and am working with one other employee here to do exactly what you mentioned – innovate within an existing institution. Not easy! But we are not giving up. There is no buy-in from the upper level admin and we have a handful of teachers who use Twitter. I’m constantly forcing myself to focus more on the culture of the organization than the tools that we want to use.

The whole CUNY crew is yet another phenomenon of innovation, and your killing it over there. Not sure what the ed startup will bring,but it’s exciting to be part of something that is so far out of my comfort zone. I am waiting patiently for CUNY to start using it’s numbers and scale to play a bit with the idea of the MOOC embedded within a university, face-to-face experience around NYC. You couldn’t ask for a better backdrop..
